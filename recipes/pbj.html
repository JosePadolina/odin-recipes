Peanut butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Spreading peanut butter and fruit preserves with jelly on bread is called a peanut butter and jelly sandwich, or PB&J. You may find sandwich made with a single slice of bread, folded over between two slices of bread, or with an openfaced sandwich in between one and two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Lay 2 pieces of bread on a plate.
  2. Open peanut butter jar and lay a thin layer of peanut butter on one of the pieces of bread.
  3. Open jelly jar and lay a thin layer of jelly on the other piece of bread.
  4. Put both pieces of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly together.
